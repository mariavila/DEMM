How to?

DEMM is an advanced IA created to help victims in emergency situations.

Data from your device is sent to a rescue team's server in order to assess the situation. You can either offer or request help, DEMM will automatically assign you a task.
Your information will help us use more efficently our resources in the event of a catastrophe.
